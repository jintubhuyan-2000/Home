About Me

I'm a passionate researcher specializing in Remote Sensing, Forest Carbon and Structural Dynamics, Forest Fire, and UAV technology. My work integrates satellite and drone data with AI techniques to understand forest ecosystems, carbon cycling, and environmental changes.

I aim to create innovative solutions for sustainable resource management and climate resilience through cutting-edge research and technology applications.
